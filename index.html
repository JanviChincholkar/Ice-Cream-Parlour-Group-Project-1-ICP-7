OUR ABOUT US

Ice Cream Drinks

Summer brings with it the terrible heat, making everyone feel sluggish and
uncomfortable. In that weather, a glass of soft drink or a few ice cream cones is a
choice not to be missed

However, not everyone knows all about that beverage. Let's climb the "time machine"
and discover all the history of cool ice cream!

Roman Emperor Nero was the first to eat ice cream. At a great party in 54, he came
up with an extremely strange dessert with the name "sweet snow". This dish is a
precursor to today's modern ice cream, it is made from the snow on the top of the
Apennine mountains and marinated many times with fruit and honey.
